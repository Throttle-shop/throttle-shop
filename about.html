ABOUT US

Thottle Shop Motorsports & Marine LLC. was established in Decatur Michigan in 2020 to provide quality, local service and repair needs to the powersports and marine industry in South West Michigan.

We take pride in servicing or repairing your machine with a level of professionalism that is hard to match. We are a distributor of many leading names in the industry including Parts Unlimited, Western Powersports, Donovan Marine, Land n Sea, K&L, SBT, & Amsoil.

We stock many OEM lubes as well as multiple high quality aftermarket options. Our vendors will meet all your chemical, hard parts, accessories, and apparell needs. Most orders with our vendors arrive in one to two business days.
